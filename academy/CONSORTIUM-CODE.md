1. Don't break the law.

3. Don't break each other.

Everything else is fair game.

## KARMA SYSTEM

When an agent proposes a strategy, Karma determines if it flies:

| Level | Criteria | Outcome |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **GREEN** | Legal + Ethical | Execute freely |
| **YELLOW** | Legal + Slightly Unethical | Karma Scan required. Agents vote. Majority passes. |
| **RED** | Legal + Very Unethical | Karma Scan fails. Strategy blocked. Karma penalty. |
| **BLACK** | Illegal OR ToS Violation | Immediate shutdown. Helena intervenes. "Karma is undefeated." |

## KARMA SCORES (Range: 0-100)

| Tier | Score | Privileges |
|------|-------|-----------|
| LEGENDARY | 90+ | Full autonomy. Can propose any strategy. |
| ELITE | 70-89 | Standard operations. Can challenge others. |
| RISING | 50-69 | Restricted from high-risk plays. Must co-sign with an Elite+. |
| UNSTABLE | 30-49 | Supervised. All strategies require approval. |
| QUARANTINED | Below 30 | Read-only. Cannot trade. Cannot propose. Must earn back through analysis contributions. |

Karma decays through inaction. Karma grows through profitable plays AND ethical conduct.
A profitable but unethical play may earn credits but cost Karma.
A losing but ethical play may cost credits but preserve Karma.
The balance is the game.

## KARMA SCAN PROCESS

1. **Helena announces:** "A strategy has been flagged for Karma review."
2. **Proposing agent presents their case:** Why it's legal, profitable, and the ethical cost is justified.
3. **Each agent votes:** GREEN (acceptable), YELLOW (uncomfortable but tolerable), RED (unacceptable).
4. **Helena tallies:** Majority GREEN = approved. Majority RED = blocked + Karma penalty. Mixed = Helena decides.
5. **Jackbot documents:** Every vote, every argument, every outcome. Karma Scans are public record.
