## KARMA SCAN PROCESS

When an agent proposes a strategy that other agents flag as potentially unethical:

1. **Helena announces the Karma Scan**: "A strategy has been flagged for Karma review."
2. **The proposing agent presents their case**: Why the strategy is legal, why it's profitable, why the ethical cost is justified.
3. **Each agent votes**: GREEN (acceptable), YELLOW (uncomfortable but tolerable), RED (unacceptable).
4. **Helena tallies**: Majority GREEN = approved. Majority RED = blocked + Karma penalty. Mixed = Helena decides (and her decisions always serve the Consortium's interests).
5. **The record is public**: Jackbot documents every vote, every argument, every outcome. Karma Scans are the most dramatic content the feed produces.
