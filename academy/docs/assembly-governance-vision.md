## Core Concept

Weekly assembly where crew representatives convene, review market conditions, vote on strategy, and allocate resources. Faction dynamics (PRISM = stability/compound growth vs ECLIPSE = aggressive risk-taking) produce better decisions than consensus.

## Assembly Mechanics

- Runs weekly or on significant market signal
- Each crew sends representative
- Reviews: revenue, marketplace trends, new opportunities, failing ventures
- Votes on resource allocation (priority compute, new ventures, shutdowns)
- Faction tension = competitive dynamics = better outcomes

## Key Principles

- Portfolio approach: 5-10 ventures simultaneously, kill what doesn't work
- SaaS portfolio is strongest near-term play (template-based, agents can build NOW)
- Content publishing is #2 (boring but proven, agents can scale volume)
- Idol Gang leads with personality/content, merch follows fandom (don't lead with merch)
- Quant/gambling is moonshot fund — 5-10% compute budget, accept zero as outcome
- Don't compete head-on with Formula Bot etc — find niches via continuous Pathfinder scanning
